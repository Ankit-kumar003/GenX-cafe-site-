A Cafe Born From a Dream

It all started with a small espresso machine and a big dream in the heart of Jaipur.

Our founder, Arjun Kapoor, spent years travelling across Europe and South America learning the craft of specialty coffee. When he returned to Jaipur, he brought with him not just beans, but a philosophy: that every cup should be an experience worth remembering.

Today, Gen X Cafe stands as Jaipur's most beloved cafe destination — a place where creative professionals, families, and coffee connoisseurs all find their tribe.

7+Years of Excellence

Our Mission

To serve exceptional coffee and food while creating a warm, inclusive space where every guest feels at home. We commit to quality ingredients, sustainable practices, and heartfelt hospitality in every interaction.

Our Vision

To become Rajasthan's most trusted cafe brand — a place synonymous with quality, community, and culture. We envision a future where Gen X Cafe is the backdrop to life's most memorable moments.
